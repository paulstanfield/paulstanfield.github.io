Paul Stanfield is a Product Designer based out of Belfast, Northern Ireland.

Hey, I’m Paul, a designer with a great passion for creating digital products and shaping user experiences. I have 4 years experience in studio, product, startup & freelance environments and have degrees in Interactive Multimedia Design and Creative Multimedia.

I believe in crafting scalable digital products that enhance user interactions and improve business performance. My process involves me identifying, researching and validating problems to design, test and ship viable solutions using various skills such as UI/UX design and front end development.

I’m fascinated by Nordic culture, brand experiences and enjoy nothing more than a tasty lunch and a good cup of coffee whilst I plan the next country to visit with my fiancée wife.

Selected companies & clients I’ve been associated with include: Simulity, Crafty Devil, Flowlens, Mette Creates, Dale Farm, Fathom & Visionworks Interactive.
