A Bit About Paul

Paul Stanfield is a designer living and working in Belfast, Northern Ireland. With degrees in Creative Multimedia and Interactive Multimedia Design, he has over three years experience in studio, startup and freelance environments. His experiences have allowed him to design and develop a range of projects with clients large and small, including Dale Farm, Compass FA, Kiverco and CDE Global. Paul currently works as a freelance designer & front end developer.

Paul offers a wide range of services from website design and development that enhances lead generation and engagement to UI/UX design for applications looking an intuitive and organic experience. He focuses on delivering high quality and professional solutions in the areas of UI/UX design, web design, front end development and branding.
